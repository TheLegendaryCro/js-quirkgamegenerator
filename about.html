About

Quirk game is similar to charades where players are given a random quirk to act out.

Quirk game is meant to be played in VRChat, but can also be used in other games.

Rules

  • It is recommended to play in 2 teams consiting of 2-4 players.
  • 1 person from each team is chosen to try and guess the rest of their teams quirks.
  • Each correctly guessed quirk gives the team 1 point.
  • Multiple rounds are played where the guessers are switched to a new member if the team.
  • Whichever team has the most points at the end wins!
